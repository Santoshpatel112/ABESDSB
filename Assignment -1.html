Welcome to ABESEC College

ABES Engineering College, established in 2000, is a premier institution located in Ghaziabad, India. Affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, it offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

About ABESEC College

ABESEC is known for its commitment to excellence in education, research, and innovation. The college boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life that fosters holistic development.

With a focus on industry-oriented learning, ABESEC collaborates with leading companies to provide students with practical exposure and internship opportunities.
